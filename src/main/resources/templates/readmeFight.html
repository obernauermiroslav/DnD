Readme Fight Page

Welcome to the arena, where your hero fights with enemy creatures.

When fighting, you can use normal attacks with button:"Fight. These attacks work like this: Your attack - enemy defence and vice versa.

But there are some enemies that have special abbilities, f.e can ignore your armor, when attacking.

And there are some special items that give you some abbilities, f.e shield can be used to block enemy attacksa

You can use various spells: "Firebolt" : will damage enemy and ignores armor, so it deals full damage.

"Heal" : will heal you up to 25 health points.

"Sunder armor": will decrease enemy armor by 3 points.

"Weakness" : will decrease enemy attack by 3 points.

You can also use "Healing potion" that will heal you up to 30 points, if you have any.

You can also hover over the "Show info" button to see additional information about each enemy.

After each victory you will be given reward and then you can fight again against stronger enemy.
