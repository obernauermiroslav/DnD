Readme Hero Page

This page shows informations abut your hero and items he has equipped and spells he has learned.

Each time you buy an item or learn new spell, new image with that specific item type or spell will appear on the right side of the page.

When you hover over that item/spell, new window with informations about that item/spell will be displayed.

You can upgrade almost all items with runes when you click on the " + " symbol next to that item.

You can upgrade most of your hero attributes with skill points when you click on the " + "symbol next to that ability

There are also buttons: "Shop" and "Fight" that, when clicked, will redirect to shop and fight page.
