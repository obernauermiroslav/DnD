Readme Shop Page

Welcome to the shop page, where you can buy various items, spells and potions for your hero if you have enough gold.

Simply click on the "Buy" button next to the item/potion/spell you want to buy, and it will be added to you inventory.

When you hover over each "Buy" button, new window with informations about that item will be displayed.

Potions can be bought without limit, each spell can be bought, but only once and you can buy only one item from each type.

There are also buttons: and "Fight" that, when clicked, will redirect to hero and fight page

Short on funds? You can try your luck with "Dice" where you roll with 2 virtual dices, and if you roll higher sum than enemy, you win.
