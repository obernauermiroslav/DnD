Readme Shop Page

Welcome to the shop page, where you can buy various items, spells and potions for your hero.

Simply click on the "Buy" button next to the item/potion/spell you want to buy, and it will be added to you inventory.

When you hover over each "Buy" button, new window with informations about that item will be displayed.

There are also buttons: "Hero" and "Fight" that, when clicked, will redirect to hero and fight page
